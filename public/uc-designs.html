Missed Delivery

Most shipments are sent via UPS. Because of state regulations, an adult (21+) signature and photo ID is required for delivery. Three attempts are made and then your shipment is returned to our fulfillment center.

Did you know?   It costs us (on average) nearly $100 in regulatory and other fees to have a package returned. To offset a portion of this cost, we charge a $35 restocking fee if you would like the bottles returned to your CloudCellar or re-shipped to you. Returned bottles cannot be refunded or exchanged for credit. We appreciate your understanding with this matter.

Change Of Delivery

You can use the UPS MyChoice delivery to schedule a delivery window or hold your package for pick-up at a UPS location. This is the best way to receive help with your delivery, because you would be working with UPS directly, thereby avoiding any possible delay.

Customer Service has limited capabilities to alter the delivery of your package. We do not have any way to cancel or change delivery across state lines.

UPS does not allow any changes to deliveries after at least one delivery attempt has been made.
