Be My Roommate?

Hello! I am looking for someone to share a luxury 2 bed 2 bath apartment in Soma. It is ideally situated on a quiet side street at the corner of Rausch & Folsom. Your space will be 1br+1ba in 2br+2ba luxury condo.

$2300/month includes utilities, private bedroom & bathroom (unfurnished), spacious closet, 1000 mbps internet (ethernet wall jack in your room + wifi throughout the unit). There is also a full size in-unit washer/dryer , high end kitchen, dishwasher, ice maker, and central HVAC (air conditioning! We'd be a match if 70-75 F is your ideal temperature range)

Please note: All applicants will be required to pass a credit and background check.
